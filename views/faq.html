What is a Contact?

A contact is a special kind of file. Inside of it, we can put things like a person's name, their phone number, their address, a picture, etc. It has a special file format with the extension '.vcf' and when you open it, the Contacts app on your phone or computer will open up, allowing you to save the new contact.
