Add your Representatives' Contacts

Step 1: Enter your Zip Code to find your reps.

Step 2: For each of your representatives, click 'Add to Contact.' This will create a contact file.

Step 3: Open the file to add your rep to your Contacts. On iOS, you may need to click Import from Contacts.

To find your representatives in your Contacts, search by name, title, or simply search 'Congress.'
